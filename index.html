If you want something you never had, do something you have never done!
Don't go the way life takes you. Take the life the way you go!
And remember .....
You are born to live and are not living because you are born!

I have been born and brought up in the beautiful & fertile Gangetic Plains of Uttar Pradesh in India and I belong to the historic city of Lucknow, which is well known for its nazaakat and nafaasat.
I did my undergraduate honours in Mathematics from Lucknow University and joined the Indian Administrative Service in its 1989 batch. As an officer, I beleieve in integrity, transparency and political neutrality.
My Passion is to strive towards better governance and the use of information technology to improve the quality and efficiency of service delivery. I am interested in following the latest trends in macroeconomics, public policy and information technology.
Life has in the real sense begun after I turned forty a few years back and I am loving it. My wellwishers are my greatest asset.
